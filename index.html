Who We Are

Driven by the need to support STEM education in the Philippines and to foster fellowship among alumni across the world, a group of alumni of the Philippine Science High School (PSHS) based in North America established a nonprofit organization called the Philippine Science High School (PSHS) International Alumni Foundation, Inc., also known as "Pisayian". Pisayian is a nonprofit organization recognized by the IRS under section 501(c)(3).

Membership is open to alumni from any campus of the PSHS system who are either currently or formerly based in North America.

Foundation Programs

Pisayian fulfills its mission through the distribution of grants and the implementation of programs aligned with supporting the PSHS system.

Program Grants

We distribute small grants to approved organizations, such as the PSHS system, the PSHS National Alumni Association (PSHSNAA), and the PSHS Foundation.
